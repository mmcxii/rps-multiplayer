The Gathering

When you hover over a color, two others are highlighted in red; those are your color's enemies. The other two colors are faded and highlighted in blue; those are your color's allies. If your opponent chooses one of your color's enemies, you get a point, if your opponent chooses one of your color's allies, they get a point. If both players choose the same color, the game results in a tie.

*Disclaimer: These are not the actual MTG color pairs. The system had to be changed to fit the rules of 5 way RPS.
